Favorite Authors

Yuval Noah Harari
- Sapiens
- 21 Rules for the 21st Century
- In my opinion, Yuval Dominates the Social philosiphy genre through his creative takes on issues through books such as Sapiens.
Rick Riordan
- The Heroes of Olympus series
- The Kane Chronicles
- Rick Riordan's works are what first developed my love for reading. Through delving into Greek, Norse, and ancient Eygptian Mythologies, Rick Riordan creates deep and creative stories with well-developed character and plotlines.
Fydor Doestevesky

- The Brothers Karmazov
